Our Story

Connecting hearts through handcrafted friendship bracelets

Our Mission

At Friendship Bracelet Studio, we believe that friendships are the colorful threads that weave together the fabric of our lives. Our mission is to create beautiful, meaningful bracelets that celebrate the unique bonds between friends, family members, and loved ones.

Every bracelet we craft tells a story of connection, love, and shared memories. We pour our hearts into each creation, ensuring that every piece is as special and unique as the relationship it represents.

Our Values

Craftsmanship

Every bracelet is meticulously handcrafted with attention to detail and quality materials.

Creativity

We embrace vibrant colors and unique designs that reflect individual personalities and styles.

Connection

We celebrate the power of friendship and the joy of giving meaningful gifts to those we love.

Sustainability

Our Journey

2018 - The Beginning

Started as a small passion project, creating bracelets for friends and family during summer camp.

2020 - Growing Community

Expanded to serve local communities, hosting bracelet-making workshops and events.

2022 - Going Digital

Launched our online platform to share the joy of friendship bracelets with people everywhere.

2024 - Today

Continuing to spread happiness one bracelet at a time, with thousands of friendships celebrated.
